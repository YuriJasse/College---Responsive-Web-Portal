Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning, committed to academic excellence, innovation, and societal transformation through quality education and research.

Our Mission

Quality Education

To provide high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers and lifelong learning.

Research & Innovation

To foster a culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.

Community Engagement

To engage with the community and industry to address societal challenges through education and research.

Program Specific Outcomes

Engineering Graduates will be able to:
  • Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering principles
  • Design and conduct experiments, analyze and interpret data
  • Design systems to meet desired needs within realistic constraints
  • Function effectively on multidisciplinary teams
  • Identify, formulate, and solve engineering problems
Business Graduates will be able to:
  • Apply business concepts and theories to solve real-world problems
  • Demonstrate effective communication and teamwork skills
  • Analyze business data and make informed decisions
  • Understand global business environments and practices
  • Apply ethical reasoning in business decision-making
